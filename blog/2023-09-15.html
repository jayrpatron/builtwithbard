Update 28. 12:41am. Something I had an inkling about has been confirmed with my experience on this website...that I cannot build it with the help of AI alone. I started the project on August 4 thinking that I will develop the site simply by prompting Google Bard to generate the right codes. The problem, especially if you're just starting out on web development, is that you need to be armed with structure and concepts, and be able to apply these in a logical way.

Simply prompting AI for codes won't do the trick. Because the right codes will come from knowing how to prompt, and evaluating the results that AI generates. To be armed with structure and concepts, one needs to follow a curriculum. This is something that FreeCodeCamp.org offers. So I go back to the drawing board, and the curriculum to better arm myself. Then perhaps I can do better prompting.
