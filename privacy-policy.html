Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the website BuiltwithBard.com.

What information do we collect?

This website collects the following information from visitors:

  • Email addresses: We collect email addresses from visitors who sign up for our newsletter.
  • IP addresses: We collect IP addresses from all visitors to our website.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to track visitors to our website and to remember their preferences.

How do we use your information?

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To send you our newsletter.
  • To track your visits to our website.
  • To remember your preferences.
  • To improve the website.
  • To prevent fraud.

Who do we share your information with?

We do not share your information with third parties, except for the following purposes:

  • To send you our newsletter, we use a third-party email service provider. This provider may have access to your email address.
  • To track your visits to our website, we use a third-party analytics service. This provider may have access to your IP address.

How do we protect your information?

We take steps to protect your information, including:

  • We store your information on secure servers.
  • We use encryption to protect your information when it is transmitted over the internet.
  • We restrict access to your information to authorized employees.

What are your rights?

You have the following rights with respect to your information:

  • You have the right to access your information.
  • You have the right to correct your information if it is inaccurate.
  • You have the right to delete your information.
  • You have the right to object to the processing of your information.
  • You have the right to withdraw your consent to the processing of your information.

How to contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on our website.
